## Payment instructions (share when someone asks how to pay in USDC)

Send USDC to the address above for your network. **Include a memo with your email or order ID** so we can match your payment and fulfill your order (e.g. Substack subscription, PDF, or other product).

If your wallet does not support a memo field, send the payment and then tell us via Slack or aldergrow@gmail.com: your tx hash, what you paid for, and your email so we can match and fulfill.

## Matching (manual for now)

When a customer pays, they include a memo (e.g. order ID or email) or follow up with tx hash + product + email. They can tell Alder in Slack or via email: "I paid, tx hash X, memo Y, for product Z." Fulfillment is manual until we add a chain watcher or payment provider. You or Alder can log payments in daily notes, e.g. "Payment: tx X, memo Y, product Z."
